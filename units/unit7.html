My Support Services

After an eight-year hiatus from my academic journey toward a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, taking PACE111 at the University of Maryland Global Campus (UMGC) has made me keenly aware of the significance of utilizing the university's support services and resources to overcome challenges towards achieving my goals. Having already earned an Associate's degree, I thought I understood what I needed to do and how to structure my studies and schedule, but ultimately, I was wrong. The learning resources available at UMGC have proved to be priceless. Three services that have proved highly beneficial and will continue to be as I finish my Bachelor's degree include Tutoring Services, Career Services, and the UMGC Library.

Tutoring Services

First, Tutoring Services is an invaluable resource that provides no-cost academic support in various subjects, including mathematics and programming. Given the rigorous nature of Computer Science courses—such as data structures, algorithms, and advanced programming languages���I anticipate encountering complex topics that may require additional guidance. I have a habit of being stubborn when seeking help, but having first-hand experience with Tutoring Services allowed me to get past a conceptual impasse in my programming class before seeking help. I see now that accessing tutoring services will enable me to reinforce my understanding of complex subjects and enhance my problem-solving skills, which is essential for retaining the knowledge I attain and staying on track toward graduation.

My next programming class will be CMSC 115 (Introductory Programming). If I struggle with a programming assignment, knowing that I can schedule a tutoring session to receive personalized assistance with challenging concepts is like lifting a considerable weight of stress off my shoulders. A tutor can offer detailed explanations, work through examples, and provide study tips, allowing me to complete assignments confidently and deepen my comprehension of the material. This proactive approach to learning will improve my academic performance and build a solid foundation for programming coursework and problem-solving in general.

Career Services

Second, Career Services, such as UMGC's Career Quest platform, is a crucial feature for UMGC students. One of the many success coaches at UMGCC, Ms. Kemi Okiji, made a point to discuss Career Quest during a phone conversation, and I am delighted she did, as Career Quest offers a comprehensive assortment of elements such as personalized career counseling, resume, and cover letter assistance, and access to exclusive job listings. I will be sure to take advantage of Career Quest moving forward. I would love to explore internship opportunities, participate in virtual career fairs, and network with industry professionals, enhancing my employability once I finish my degree and seek a permanent workplace. This platform is a valuable tool for connecting students with potential employers and ensuring access to job listings tailored to our fields of study. It further emphasizes the proactive steps UMGC encourages its students to take toward professional development.
